Velocity Fitness Social Media

Velocity Fitness came to us with a struggling social media presence. With only 2,000 followers and minimal engagement, they needed a complete social strategy overhaul.

The Challenge

Inconsistent posting, low-quality content, and no clear strategy resulted in poor engagement and difficulty attracting new gym members through social channels.

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive content strategy including daily workout videos, member transformations, nutrition tips, motivational content, and community engagement. All content was professionally filmed and edited.
