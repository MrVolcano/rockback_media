Artisan Brew Co. AI Chatbot

Artisan Brew Co., a specialty coffee roaster, was drowning in customer service inquiries. Their small team couldn't keep up with questions about products, orders, and brewing methods.

The Challenge

Limited staff spending 60% of their time answering repetitive customer questions via email and social media, leaving little time for business growth and product development.

Our Solution

We developed a custom AI chatbot trained on their product catalog, brewing guides, and FAQs. The chatbot integrates with their website and handles product recommendations, order tracking, brewing advice, and general inquiries 24/7.

The Results

85%

Inquiries Automated

24/7

Availability

The team now focuses on complex issues and business growth while the AI handles routine questions. Customer satisfaction increased due to instant responses.
