Summit Realty Video Campaign

Summit Realty Group wanted to revolutionize their property listings with cinematic video tours featuring stunning aerial drone footage and professional interior videography.

The Challenge

High-end properties were being shown with low-quality phone photos and basic walkthroughs. Buyers expected premium visual content that matched the caliber of the properties.

Our Solution

We created a complete video production system including 4K drone cinematography, interior videography, professional editing, and custom music. Each property received a 2-3 minute cinematic showcase.
