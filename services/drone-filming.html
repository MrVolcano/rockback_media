Our Process

1

Planning

We scout locations, check airspace regulations, obtain necessary permits, and plan optimal flight paths. Weather and lighting conditions are carefully considered.

2

Flight Operations

Our licensed pilots execute the flight plan with precision, capturing stunning footage from multiple angles while prioritizing safety and regulatory compliance.

3

Post-Production

Raw footage is color graded, stabilized, and edited to perfection. We combine aerial shots with ground footage if needed to create a cohesive final product.

4

Delivery

You receive fully edited 4K or 6K video files and high-resolution images, optimized for your specific platform and use case. All raw footage is archived.
